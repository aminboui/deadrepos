## Recording Tips

### Camera Movements
1. **Slow orbits** - show off the 3D scene
2. **Zoom to details** - graves, trees, moon
3. **Smooth transitions** - no jerky movements

### Audio
- Use calm, slightly spooky background music
- Clear narration (use good microphone)
- Keep sound effects audible but not overwhelming

### Lighting
- Record at night or in dark room for authentic spooky feel
- Screen should be bright and clear

### Editing
- Add subtle transitions between sections
- Include text overlays for key features
- Add GitHub/deployment URLs at the end
- Keep it under 3 minutes!

### What to Emphasize
1. **Visual polish** - This is Costume Contest category
2. **Atmospheric effects** - Moon, particles, trees, fog
3. **Interactive elements** - Hover, click, AI analysis
4. **Technical sophistication** - Three.js, AI integration
5. **Kiro usage** - Show .kiro directory briefly

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Don't spend too long on setup
- Don't show errors or bugs
- Don't talk too fast
- Don't forget to show the AI jokes (they're fun!)
- Don't skip the atmospheric effects
