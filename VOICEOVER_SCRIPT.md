## Recording Notes

- **Record in a quiet room** with good acoustics
- **Use a quality microphone** (even a decent USB mic works)
- **Stand while recording** for better energy
- **Smile while talking** - it comes through in your voice
- **Record multiple takes** of each section
- **Leave pauses** between sections for easier editing
- **Record room tone** (30 seconds of silence) for audio cleanup
